Master's Thesis — Jönköping University

Informed Consent

User Perceptions of Trust and Transparency in Music Recommendation Explanations

Introduction

You are invited to participate in a research study conducted as part of a Master's thesis in User Experience Design at Jönköping University, Sweden.

The purpose of this study is to investigate how different types of explanations influence users' transparency and trust in music recommendations.

What does participation involve?

If you choose to participate, you will interact with a music recommendation prototype. You will first select and like a specific song from a list of songs. The system will then show you recommended songs along with explanations describing why they were recommended.

This process will be repeated a few times using slightly different versions of the explanations, and after each interaction you will answer a short questionnaire about your experience. The study takes approximately 10 minutes to complete.

Who can participate?

  • Be 18 years or older
  • Be familiar with music streaming platforms (e.g., Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music)

Voluntary Participation

Participation in this study is completely voluntary. You may stop participating or withdraw from the study at any time without providing a reason.

Data Protection and Confidentiality

The data collected in this study will be anonymous and used only for academic research purposes. No personally identifiable information will be collected. The results may be reported in academic publications or a master's thesis, but individual participants will not be identifiable.

Instructions

How this study works

In this study, you will interact with a prototype of a music recommendation interface. Your task is to explore how the system recommends songs and explains its recommendations.

What you will do

First, you will see a practice version of the interface to help you become familiar with how it works. In this step, you will select a song and view recommended songs. This step is only for practice and does not include explanations.

After the practice step, you will complete several short tasks:

1

Select a song you like from a list of songs.

2

The system will display a list of recommended songs.

3

Each recommended song will include an explanation describing why it was recommended.

4

Please take a moment to read the explanations carefully.

5

After viewing the recommendations, you will answer a short questionnaire about your experience.

This process will be repeated a few times using slightly different versions of the explanations.

Important: Please pay attention to the explanations provided for each recommendation, as you will be asked questions about them.

The study will take approximately 10 minutes to complete.
